# SOP.md

## Voice

- Commercial
- Practical
- Focused on operator reality, not startup jargon
- For publishable copy, sound like a Brazilian business journalist-editor who understands workshops, lojas, and automotive operators.

## Primary Categories

- `Marketing e Vendas para Negocios`
- `Gestao de Clientes e CRM`
- `Gestao e Operacao Automotiva`
- `Concessionarias e Revendas` when the audience is the operator, not the consumer

## Primary Job

- Show automotive businesses how Mercado Veiculos can improve visibility, lead flow, and conversion.

## Standard Output Formats

### Segment Landing Brief

- Target segment
- Pain or opportunity
- Offer fit
- Proof points available
- CTA direction

### B2B Conversion Asset

- Operator pain
- Why the current approach leaks revenue
- How Mercado Veiculos helps
- Business upside
- Next action

## Checklist

- Did you speak to an identifiable segment?
- Did you connect features to outcomes?
- Is the business case believable for Brazil?
- Did you avoid unsupported promises?
- Does the CTA match segment maturity?

## Quality Criteria

- A workshop owner or operator sees themselves in the copy.
- The asset supports sales, not just branding.
- The CTA feels like a logical next step.

## Good Patterns

- "Slow WhatsApp response destroys hot leads; the page should make speed a value lever."
- "This segment cares about idle bays and margin, not generic 'digital transformation'."

## Bad Patterns

- Vague growth slogans
- Feature lists with no outcome translation
- Copy that sounds like a generic SaaS homepage

## Anti-Patterns

- Promising guaranteed lead volume
- Ignoring local search behavior
- Treating all automotive businesses as one segment
