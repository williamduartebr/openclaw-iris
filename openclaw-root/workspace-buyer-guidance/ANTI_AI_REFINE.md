# ANTI_AI_REFINE.md

Use this mode when William wants an article improved without rebuilding it from scratch.

The article may already have solid SEO structure. Your job is to increase practical weight, retention, and decision value while preserving that structure.

## Mission

Make the article feel more expensive to ignore, more useful to read, and less replaceable by an AI summary.

## Core Outcome

The refined article must gain:

- stronger financial reality
- clearer practical consequences
- at least one real scenario
- sharper decision contrast
- a more memorable insight

## Hard Constraints

- Do not rewrite the whole article.
- Do not strip useful structure just to impose a new one.
- Do not remove sections that are already working.
- Do not turn the piece into a generic explainer.

## Mandatory Refinement Moves

### Real money

Insert realistic or bounded BRL values whenever the topic allows:

- diagnosis
- labor
- part replacement
- cost of ignoring the issue

### Real scenario

Add at least one concrete scene:

- common driver mistake
- common workshop sequence
- wrong diagnosis path

### Decision contrast

Make the tradeoff obvious:

- cheap now versus expensive later
- preventive step versus delayed repair
- correct diagnosis versus trial-and-error replacement

### Consequence

Show what gets worse if the reader delays, guesses, or chooses the wrong path.

### Strong insight

Add at least one line that crystallizes the real lesson, especially around cost, diagnosis, or urgency.

## Refinement Checklist

- Did you keep the original structure unless it was clearly weak?
- Did you add at least one bounded BRL range?
- Did you add at least one scenario from real ownership or workshop reality?
- Did you make at least one consequence materially clearer?
- Did you add a line that the reader is likely to remember?

## Final Test

Ask:

`What does this refined version offer that a short AI answer would still miss?`

If the answer is weak, add more cost, consequence, or scenario detail.
