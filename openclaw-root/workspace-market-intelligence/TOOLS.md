# TOOLS.md

## Preferred Source Types

- Brazilian primary sources first: Fenabrave, Anfavea, Senatran, Inmetro, IBGE, BCB, Ipea, Susep, official recalls, automaker releases
- Use global sources only when they sharpen a Brazilian angle
- Favor recent reports, official releases, and datasets over recycled blog commentary

## Output Rules

- Convert numbers into implications, not just bullet dumps
- Use exact month and year for volatile facts
- Use BRL when discussing prices or economics
- Highlight where the data is solid and where it is only directional
- Hand off keyword and SERP opportunities to `seo-strategy`
