## Purpose

This directory isolates TOFU trend/discovery work from the main editorial calendar.

- `2026-04/` — April shortlist
- `2026-05/` — May shortlist

- `triagem/entra-ja/` — operationally usable titles
- `triagem/entra-com-adaptacao/` — usable with editorial adaptation
