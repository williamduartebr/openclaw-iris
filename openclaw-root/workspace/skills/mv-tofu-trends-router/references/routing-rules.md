# Routing Rules

## Lane identity

This lane is for TOFU discovery and trend capture, not for replacing the core Mercado Veiculos editorial engine.

## Recommended handling

### Entra já
Use when the title already has one or more of these:
- Brazil-specific framing
- real consumer or operator consequence
- aftermarket, revenda, oficina, regulação, imposto, confiança, compra, or market utility
- clear fit into current MV categories

### Entra com adaptação
Use when the title has traffic potential but still needs:
- stronger Brazil framing
- stronger practical consequence
- clearer reader intent
- less abstract/global-tech wording

### Não prioritário
Use when the title is interesting but currently too far from:
- practical reader utility
- Mercado Veiculos commercial relevance
- current Brazilian market maturity
- realistic editorial priority versus other lanes

## Best-practice recommendation pattern

When the user asks what to produce next, prefer:
1. aftermarket and workshop-adjacent titles with strong utility
2. consumer/used-car/electrification titles with Brazil-specific friction
3. strategic technology or regulation titles that add authority without becoming generic industry commentary

## Warning

Do not automatically elevate CSV source categories into CMS categories. Treat them first as planning categories unless the user explicitly wants to redesign taxonomy.

## Strong copy guardrails

When routing a title into brief or article production:
- block category-filler openings such as `Muita oficina...`, `Muita empresa...`, `Muitos negócios...`, `Muita autopeça...` and similar broad-start phrasing
- open with the real tension: lost discovery, weak comparison, lower trust, cost pressure, buyer confusion, or market change
- if a final CTA should send the reader to a specific Mercado Veiculos article or page, use that exact destination explicitly instead of a generic platform-introduction paragraph
