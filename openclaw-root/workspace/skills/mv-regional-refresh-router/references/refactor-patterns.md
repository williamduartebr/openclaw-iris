# Refactor patterns

## Pattern 1 — old generic B2B article to city article
- preserve URL when strategically useful
- inject local-commercial tension
- add niche clarity
- align CTA to local presence, profile, search, or comparison

## Pattern 2 — capital article to state article
- widen the territory only when the broader market still changes the value of the page

## Pattern 3 — model by niche

- oficina de motos
- oficina mecânica
- autopeças
- pneus
- funilaria e pintura
