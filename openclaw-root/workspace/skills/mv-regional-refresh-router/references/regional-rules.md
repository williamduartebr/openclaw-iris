# Regional rules

## Core identity
This lane exists to scale Mercado Veiculos through territorial editorial assets.

## Formula
Always think in:
- niche
- territory
- intent

## Territory quality test
A regional article is worth doing when the territory changes:
- the search behavior
- the comparison behavior
- the friction to convert
- the local-commercial reading of the problem

## Do not do
- city swap without editorial change
- state swap without local-commercial consequence
- near-duplicate metadata with toponyms rotated
