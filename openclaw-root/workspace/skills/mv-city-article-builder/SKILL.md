# MV City Article Builder

## Overview

Create city-specific Mercado Veiculos articles in pt-BR that feel local, commercially sharp, and genuinely useful for business owners. The article should be hard to replace with a generic summary because it carries local urgency, real choice friction, and a clear next action.

Read `references/city-patterns.md` when choosing city fit, local framing, CTA wording, or next-article suggestions.

## Workflow

1. Confirm the niche and city.
2. Frame the commercial problem in that city: comparison speed, trust gap, urgency, discovery, dependence on indicação, weak presence, or contact friction.
3. Build an article architecture unique to that request.
4. Write with local-commercial specificity instead of generic category talk.
5. Insert the required CTA sequence with dynamic copy adapted to the niche and city.
6. Review for repetition and anti-template quality.
7. Suggest next city articles with high local search potential.

## City fit

Prefer city-level scope when:
- the query has clear local-commercial intent
- the service is chosen near the moment of need
- comparison by bairro, proximidade, região urbana, or urgência matters
- the user explicitly names a city

If the city is weak for the topic, say so and suggest a stronger city or a state-level article instead.

## Writing standard

- Write for the business owner or manager.
- Do not mention AI, anti-AI logic, prompt strategy, or editorial engineering.
- Do not open with repeated scaffolds like `Muita empresa...`, `Muita oficina...`, `Para muita empresa...`, or similar filler.
- Do not recycle the same H2 ladder across adjacent city articles.
- Make the city matter in the reasoning, not only in the title.
- Use local-commercial language: bairro, região, urgência, comparação, contato, confiança, orçamento, agenda, descoberta.
- Prefer concrete local tension over broad explanation.
- Keep every article distinct in lead, examples, section order, and CTA bridge.
