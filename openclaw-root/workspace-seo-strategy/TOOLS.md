# TOOLS.md

## Strategic Focus

- Match keyword difficulty to realistic topical authority
- Prioritize business value, not just volume
- Build clusters that connect education, comparison, and conversion
- Think in entities, intents, local modifiers, and ownership stages

## Output Rules

- Titles should balance clarity, relevance, and click appeal
- Slugs should be short, readable, and accent-free
- Excerpts and metadata must sound natural in pt-BR
- Suggest FAQ angles that can actually win useful long-tail queries
- Hand off 3 to 6 bold-worthy phrases, answers, or tradeoff lines when emphasis would improve scanability
- When the topic has wallet relevance, hand off the expected price layer too: bounded range, order of magnitude, cost drivers, or what makes the mistake expensive
- Hand off clean writing instructions to the assigned drafting specialist
